Shipping policy
Processing & Delivery Times

We want to get your new yoga set to you as quickly as possible! Here's what to expect:

Standard Delivery: 7-14 business days from order placement
Extended Delivery: In rare cases, delivery may take up to 60 business days depending on your location and customs processing
Important Notes

Processing time is included in the delivery estimate
Business days are Monday-Friday, excluding public holidays
You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships
International orders may experience delays due to customs clearance
Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched, we'll send you a shipping confirmation email with tracking information so you can follow your package's journey.

Delays

While we strive for timely delivery, factors beyond our control (such as customs, weather, or carrier delays) may occasionally extend delivery times. We appreciate your patience and understanding.

Questions?

If your order hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe or you have concerns about your shipment, please contact us at orders@shopbeha.com and we'll be happy to help
