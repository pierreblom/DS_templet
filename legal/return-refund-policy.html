1. Introduction

At ShopBeha, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Return and Refund Policy explains your rights and our procedures in accordance with the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008 (CPA).

2. Your Consumer Rights (CPA Compliance)

2.1 Cooling-Off Period (CPA Section 16)

You have the right to return any product within 7 (seven) days of receiving it, without providing a reason.

Important Conditions:

  • Products must be in original, unused condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • Hygiene seals must be unbroken (for intimate apparel)
  • You are responsible for return shipping costs
  • Original delivery costs are not refunded

2.2 Defective Products (CPA Section 20 & 56)

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, you have the right to:

  • A full refund (including delivery costs)
  • A replacement product at no additional cost
  • Repair (if applicable and agreed)

We cover all return shipping costs for defective or incorrect items.

2.3 Warranty Period

Products are covered against manufacturing defects for 30 days from the date of receipt.

3. Eligibility for Returns

3.1 Returnable Products

The following products can be returned within 7 days if conditions are met:

  • All bras with hygiene seal intact
  • All shapewear with hygiene seal intact
  • All apparel with original tags attached
  • All products in unused, resalable condition

3.2 Non-Returnable Products

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following cannot be returned once packaging/seals are broken:

  • Intimate Apparel: If hygiene seal is broken and item is not defective
  • Final Sale Items: Clearly marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
  • Gift Cards: Non-returnable once issued
  • Worn or Washed Items: Unless defective

4. How to Return a Product

Step 1: Contact Us
Email us at returns@shopbeha.com within 7 days of receiving your order.

Include: Order number, product name(s), reason for return, photos (if defective)
Step 2: Receive Return Authorisation
We will respond within 2 business days with a Return Authorisation Number (RAN) and return instructions.
Step 3: Package Your Return
Include RAN in package, secure packaging, include all original items and tags.
Step 4: Ship Your Return
Use a trackable courier service and keep proof of postage.
Step 5: Refund Processing
Inspection within 1-2 days, refund processed within 7 business days.

5. Refund Process

5.1 Refund Amount

Change of Mind Returns:

  • Product price is refunded
  • Original delivery costs are NOT refunded
  • Return shipping costs are your responsibility

Defective/Incorrect Items:

  • Full product price refunded
  • Original delivery costs refunded
  • Return shipping costs refunded or prepaid

5.2 Refund Timeline

Return shipment to us1-5 days (depending on courier)
Inspection1-2 business days after receipt

6. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange a product:

  1. Return the original product (follow return process above)
  2. Place a new order for the desired product

7. Damaged or Defective Products

Report damaged or defective products within 7 days of receipt:

Email: returns@shopbeha.com
Subject: "Damaged/Defective Product - Order #[Order Number]"
Include: Order number, product details, clear photos showing defect or damage

We will review your claim within 2 business days and provide prepaid return shipping if return is required.
