## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website:
- Remember your preferences
- Understand how you use the site
- Improve your experience
- Provide personalised content

### 2.1 First-Party vs Third-Party Cookies

- **First-Party Cookies:** Set by ShopBeha directly
- **Third-Party Cookies:** Set by external services we use (e.g., payment processors, analytics)

### 2.2 Session vs Persistent Cookies

- **Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them

## 4. Types of Cookies We Use

**Purpose:** Essential for website operation

## 6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 6.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner offering choices:
- **Accept All Cookies:** Enables all cookies for optimal experience
- **Reject Non-Essential:** Only essential cookies are enabled
- **Customise Settings:** Choose specific cookie categories

### 6.2 Changing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time:

**Option 1: Cookie Settings Link**
- Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer
- Update your preferences
- Save changes

**Option 2: Browser Settings**
- Use your browser's cookie management tools (see Section 6.4)

**Option 3: Contact Us**
- Email: privacy@shopbeha.com
- Request specific cookies be disabled for your account

### 6.3 What Happens When You Disable Cookies

| Cookie Type | Impact if Disabled |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Essential | Website may not function properly; shopping cart, checkout, and login will not work |
| Performance | We cannot improve website based on usage data; no impact on functionality |
| Functionality | Preferences not saved; need to re-select language, currency, etc. each visit |
| Marketing | No personalised recommendations; fewer relevant ads; no impact on core shopping |

### 6.4 Browser Cookie Controls

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

**Google Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose cookie settings
3. Manage exceptions

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy
2. Manage cookies and website data
3. Block or allow cookies

**Firefox:**
1. Options → Privacy & Security
2. Cookies and Site Data
3. Manage permissions

**Microsoft Edge:**
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
2. Manage and delete cookies
3. Block or allow cookies

**Mobile Browsers:**
- Settings → Privacy → Block Cookies

**Complete Browser Guide:** [All About Cookies](https://www.allaboutcookies.org/)

## 7. Other Tracking Technologies

### 7.1 Web Beacons (Pixels)

We may use web beacons (small transparent images) in:
- Emails to track opens and clicks
- Website pages to track page views
- Advertisements to track effectiveness

**Purpose:** Measure campaign performance and user engagement

### 7.2 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:
- Cache product information for faster loading
- Store temporary data for better performance
- Remember non-sensitive preferences

**Difference from Cookies:** Local storage persists longer and stores more data but is not sent to our servers with each request.

### 7.3 Device Fingerprinting

We may collect technical information about your device for:
- Fraud prevention
- Security purposes
- Troubleshooting technical issues

**Information Collected:**
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Screen resolution
- Time zone
- Installed plugins

**Purpose:** Security and fraud detection only; not used for advertising

## 8. POPIA Compliance

### 8.1 Lawful Processing

We process cookie data based on:
- **Consent:** You explicitly consent to non-essential cookies
- **Legitimate Interest:** Essential cookies for website functionality
- **Contractual Necessity:** Cookies required to fulfil your order

### 8.2 Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Know what cookie data we collect
- **Correction:** Update inaccurate cookie preferences
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of non-essential cookies
- **Objection:** Object to processing of non-essential cookies
- **Restriction:** Limit processing to essential cookies only

**Exercise Your Rights:** Email privacy@shopbeha.com

### 8.3 Data Retention

| Cookie Type | Retention Period |
|-------------|------------------|
| Essential | Session to 7 days |
| Performance | Up to 2 years |
| Functionality | Up to 1 year |
| Marketing | Up to 1 year |

After expiry, cookies are automatically deleted.

### 8.4 International Data Transfers

Some third-party cookies may transfer data outside South Africa:
- **Google (USA):** EU-US Data Privacy Framework certified
- **Meta (USA):** Standard contractual clauses in place
- **Stripe (USA):** Adequate safeguards implemented

We ensure all transfers comply with POPIA requirements.
