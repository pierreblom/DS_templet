## 3. Electronic Transactions Process (ECTA Section 43(1))

### 3.1 How Electronic Transactions Work

When you purchase from ShopBeha:

**Step 1: Browse and Select**
- You view products on our website
- You select size, colour, and quantity
- You add products to your shopping cart

**Step 2: Checkout Initiation**
- You proceed to checkout
- You review cart contents and pricing
- You may create an account or checkout as guest

**Step 3: Information Provision**
- You provide delivery address
- You provide contact information
- You select delivery method
- Delivery cost is calculated and displayed

**Step 4: Order Review**
- You review complete order summary including:
  - Product details (name, size, colour, quantity)
  - Individual prices
  - Subtotal
  - Delivery cost
  - VAT breakdown
  - **Total amount payable**

**Step 5: Payment**
- You select payment method
- You are redirected to secure payment processor (Stripe)
- You provide payment details on secure platform
- Payment is authorised

**Step 6: Order Confirmation**
- You receive on-screen confirmation
- You receive email confirmation (data message) containing:
  - Order number
  - Order details
  - Total amount paid
  - Delivery address
  - Expected delivery timeframe

**Step 7: Fulfilment**
- We process your order
- We dispatch your products
- You receive tracking information
- Products are delivered to specified address

### 3.2 When Is the Contract Formed?

The contract is formed when:
- You submit your order ("Place Order" button)
- We accept your order (via email confirmation)
- Payment is successfully authorised

**Important:** Order submission constitutes an offer. We reserve the right to decline orders. The contract is only binding upon our acceptance.

### 3.3 Cooling-Off Period (ECTA Section 44(1)(g))

You have a **7 (seven) day cooling-off period** from the date you receive the goods, during which you may:
- Return products without providing a reason
- Receive a full refund (excluding delivery costs)

**Conditions Apply:** See our [Return and Refund Policy](./return-refund-policy.md)

## 4. Pricing and Payment Terms (ECTA Section 43(1))

### 4.1 Pricing

**All prices displayed on our website:**
- Are in South African Rand (ZAR)
- Include Value-Added Tax (VAT) at 15%
- Are subject to change without prior notice
- Are valid at the time of order confirmation
- Are clearly displayed on product pages and at checkout

**Price Breakdown at Checkout:**
```

```

### 4.2 Payment Methods Accepted

We accept the following payment methods through our secure payment processor, Stripe:

- **Credit Cards:** Visa, Mastercard
- **Debit Cards:** Visa Debit, Mastercard Debit
- **Instant EFT:** Where available
- **Additional methods:** As made available through Stripe

### 4.3 Payment Processing

- **Processor:** Stripe, Inc.
- **Security:** PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant
- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 (minimum TLS 1.2)
- **Data Storage:** We do not store full card details
- **Payment Timing:** Payment is authorised immediately; charged upon dispatch

### 4.4 Failed Payments

If payment fails:
- Your order is not confirmed
- You will receive notification via email
- You may retry payment within 24 hours
- After 24 hours, the order is automatically cancelled

### 4.5 Price Errors

If we discover a pricing error:
- Before dispatch: We will contact you to confirm or cancel
- After dispatch: We honour the incorrect price
