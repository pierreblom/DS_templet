## 2. Delivery Coverage

### 2.1 Delivery Areas

We deliver to **all addresses within South Africa**, including:
- Residential addresses
- Business addresses
- Post Office boxes (selected couriers only)
- Postal pickup points
- Pargo collection points

### 2.2 Areas We Currently Do Not Deliver To

- **International addresses** (outside South Africa)
- **Military bases** (restricted access areas)
- **Remote locations** without courier service coverage

If you're unsure whether we deliver to your area, contact us before placing your order.

### 2.3 Delivery Address Requirements

Please ensure your delivery address includes:
- Full street address (no P.O. Box unless specified as Pargo point)
- Suburb/Area name
- City/Town
- Province
- Postal code
- Contact telephone number
- Special delivery instructions (if applicable)

**Important:** You are responsible for providing accurate delivery information. We cannot be held liable for delays or non-delivery due to incorrect addresses.

## 3. Shipping Methods and Costs

### 3.1 Standard Delivery

**Delivery Time:** 3-7 business days (depending on location)

**Cost:**
- Orders under R500: R65 - R95 (calculated at checkout based on location)
- Orders R500 and above: **FREE**

### 3.2 Express Delivery (Coming Soon)

**Delivery Time:** 1-2 business days (major centres only)  
**Cost:** R150 - R200 (calculated at checkout)

**Availability:** Gauteng, Cape Town, Durban (metropolitan areas only)

### 3.3 Calculation of Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on:
- Delivery address location
- Order weight and dimensions
- Selected delivery speed
- Current courier rates

**The exact shipping cost is displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.**

### 3.4 Free Shipping Promotions

- **Standard:** Free shipping on all orders over R500
- **Special Promotions:** Watch for limited-time free shipping offers

**Note:** Free shipping applies to standard delivery only. Express delivery fees will apply even if order qualifies for free standard shipping.

## 4. Processing Times

### 4.1 Order Processing

**Standard Processing Time:** 1-2 business days

During this time, we:
- Verify payment
- Pick and pack your products
- Conduct quality checks
- Generate shipping labels
- Hand over to courier

### 4.2 Factors Affecting Processing Time

Processing may take longer during:
- Public holidays
- Peak shopping seasons (Black Friday, festive season)
- Stock verification delays
- Payment verification issues

**We will notify you via email if any delays are expected.**

### 4.3 Cut-Off Times

Orders placed:
- **Before 14:00 (Monday - Friday):** Processed same business day
- **After 14:00:** Processed next business day
- **Weekends and Public Holidays:** Processed on next business day

## 7. Order Tracking

### 7.1 Tracking Information

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive:
- **Shipping confirmation email** containing:
  - Courier name
  - Tracking number/Waybill number
  - Link to courier tracking page
  - Estimated delivery date

### 7.2 How to Track Your Order

**Option 1: ShopBeha Website**
- Log in to your account
- Go to "My Orders"
- Click "Track Order"

**Option 2: Courier Website**
- Use the tracking number from your email
- Visit the courier's tracking page
- Enter your tracking number

**Option 3: Contact Us**
- Email: support@shopbeha.com
- Provide your order number
- We will provide tracking update

### 7.3 Tracking Updates

Tracking typically updates:
- **First Scan:** When courier collects from our warehouse
- **In Transit:** As package moves between depots
- **Out for Delivery:** On the day of delivery
- **Delivered:** When package is signed for

**Note:** Tracking may not update in real-time. Allow 6-12 hours between scans.

### 7.4 Tracking Issues

If tracking has not updated within 2 business days of dispatch:
- Contact us at support@shopbeha.com
- We will investigate with the courier
- We will provide an update within 1 business day

## 8. Delivery Process

### 8.1 Delivery Attempts

Couriers typically make:
- **1-2 delivery attempts** to your address
- **Contact via phone** if unable to access property
- **Leave notification card** if nobody home

### 8.2 Signature Requirements

Most deliveries require a signature:
- Someone over 18 must be available to sign
- ID may be requested for high-value orders
- Authority to leave (ATL) may be arranged (see below)

### 8.3 Authority to Leave (Safe Place)

If nobody will be home:
- Leave instructions in "Delivery Notes" at checkout
- Examples: "Leave with security guard", "Leave at front door", "Under carport"
- **You assume responsibility for packages left without signature**

### 8.4 Failed Delivery

If delivery fails:
- **First Attempt:** Second attempt scheduled
- **Second Attempt:** Package held at depot or collection point
- **Notification:** SMS/Email sent with collection details
- **Hold Period:** 5-7 business days before return to sender

### 8.5 Collection from Depot

If you need to collect from courier depot:
- Bring tracking number
- Bring ID document
- Visit during courier's operating hours
- Contact courier directly to confirm address

## 9. Pargo Pickup Points

### 9.1 What is Pargo?

Pargo is a network of pickup points at retail stores nationwide where you can collect your order at your convenience.

### 9.2 Benefits

- **Convenient:** Pick up when it suits you
- **Secure:** No risk of porch theft
- **Flexible hours:** Most locations open 7 days a week
- **Hold period:** Up to 7 days to collect

### 9.3 How It Works

1. **Select Pargo at checkout:** Choose nearest pickup point
2. **Order dispatched:** Same as standard delivery
3. **Notification:** SMS + Email when order arrives at pickup point
4. **Collection:** Visit pickup point with ID and tracking number
5. **Sign and collect:** Within 7 days

### 9.4 Pickup Point Hours

Most Pargo points are located at:
- Supermarkets
- Retail stores
- Petrol stations

**Typical hours:** 08:00 - 20:00 (7 days a week)

Check specific hours for your selected pickup point.

## 10. Delivery Issues and Resolution

### 10.1 Damaged Packages

If your package arrives damaged:
- **Do not refuse delivery** (accept and inspect)
- **Take photos** of damaged packaging and product
- **Contact us immediately** at support@shopbeha.com
- **Report within 48 hours** of delivery

We will:
- Investigate the issue
- File claim with courier
- Provide replacement or refund

### 10.2 Missing Items

If items are missing from your order:
- Check packaging thoroughly
- Verify packing slip against order confirmation
- Contact us within 48 hours at support@shopbeha.com

We will:
- Review your order records
- Ship missing items immediately
- Issue refund if items unavailable

### 10.3 Incorrect Address

If you realise you provided the wrong address:
- **Before dispatch:** Contact us immediately to update
- **After dispatch:** Contact us ASAP; we'll try to redirect
- **Additional fees may apply** for address changes after dispatch

### 10.4 Order Not Received

If your order shows "Delivered" but you didn't receive it:

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Check with household members/neighbours
2. Check safe place (if ATL was arranged)
3. Check security/reception (if business address)
4. Verify delivery address in order confirmation

**Contact Us:**
- Email: support@shopbeha.com within 48 hours
- Provide: Order number, tracking number, delivery address

**Our Investigation:**
- Contact courier for proof of delivery (POD)
- Request delivery photo/signature
- File claim if confirmed not received
- Reship or refund if confirmed lost

**Timeframe:** 7-10 business days for investigation

### 10.5 Delayed Deliveries

If delivery exceeds estimated timeframe:
- Check tracking for updates
- Contact us after 2 business days past estimate
- We will investigate and provide update within 24 hours

**We are not liable for courier delays but will assist in resolution.**

## 11. Special Delivery Instructions

### 11.1 Providing Instructions

Add special instructions at checkout in "Delivery Notes" field:

**Examples:**
- "Call on arrival, security does not have access"
- "Deliver to reception, 3rd floor"
- "Gate code: 1234#"
- "Leave with neighbour at #15 if not home"

### 11.2 Business Address Deliveries

For business addresses, include:
- Company name
- Building name/number
- Floor/Suite number
- Reception contact number
- Business hours

### 11.3 Complex/Estate Deliveries

For complexes or estates:
- Provide access details/gate codes
- Specify unit/apartment number clearly
- Include alternative contact if security needs authorisation
- Inform security/management of expected delivery

### 11.4 Limitations

Couriers may not:
- Enter homes or unlocked premises
- Unpack or install products
- Wait more than 5 minutes for collection
- Deliver to third parties without authorisation

## 12. Public Holidays and Peak Seasons

### 12.1 Public Holiday Impact

- **No processing** on public holidays
- **No deliveries** on public holidays
- **Processing resumes** on next business day

**Plan ahead:** Order at least 5 business days before public holidays.

### 12.2 Peak Seasons

Expect delays during:

- **Festive Season** (Mid-December to early January)
- **Valentine's Day** (Early February)
- **Mother's Day** (May)

**During peak seasons:**
- Processing may take 3-5 business days
- Delivery may take 7-14 business days
- Order early to avoid disappointment

### 12.3 Festive Season Closure

We typically close between:
- **24 December - 2 January**

**Exact dates announced annually on our website.**

Orders placed during closure:
- Are not processed until we reopen
- Will be notified via email of expected processing date
