3. Electronic Transactions Process (ECTA Section 43(1))

3.1 How Electronic Transactions Work

When you purchase from ShopBeha:

Step 1: Browse and Select
You view products, select size/colour/quantity, and add to cart
Step 2: Checkout Initiation
You proceed to checkout and review cart contents
Step 3: Information Provision
You provide delivery address, contact information, and select delivery method
Step 4: Order Review
You review complete order summary including total amount payable
Step 5: Payment
You select payment method and authorise payment via Stripe
Step 6: Order Confirmation
You receive on-screen and email confirmation with order details
Step 7: Fulfilment
We process, dispatch, and deliver your order with tracking information

3.2 When Is the Contract Formed?

The contract is formed when:

  • You submit your order ("Place Order" button)
  • We accept your order (via email confirmation)
  • Payment is successfully authorised

Important: Order submission constitutes an offer. We reserve the right to decline orders. The contract is only binding upon our acceptance.

3.3 Cooling-Off Period (ECTA Section 44(1)(g))

You have a 7 (seven) day cooling-off period from the date you receive the goods, during which you may return products without providing a reason.

Conditions Apply: See our Return and Refund Policy
