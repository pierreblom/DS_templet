## üáøüá¶ South African Legal Compliance

These documents ensure compliance with:

### Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)
- **Act Number:** 4 of 2013
- **Key Sections:** 9-18 (Conditions for lawful processing)
- **Documents:** Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy

### Consumer Protection Act (CPA)
- **Act Number:** 68 of 2008
- **Key Sections:** Section 16 (cooling-off), Section 20 (returns), Section 56 (defective goods)
- **Documents:** Terms and Conditions, Return and Refund Policy

### Electronic Communications and Transactions Act (ECTA)
- **Act Number:** 25 of 2002
- **Key Sections:** Section 43 (disclosure requirements), Section 44 (consumer rights)
- **Documents:** ECTA Disclosure, Terms and Conditions

## üîê POPIA Information Officer

**Required by POPIA Section 55:**

Appoint an Information Officer responsible for:
- Ensuring POPIA compliance
- Handling data subject requests
- Maintaining records of processing activities
- Serving as contact point for Information Regulator

**Details to complete:**
- Information Officer name
- Contact details
- Responsibilities documented

## üìä Record Keeping (POPIA Section 51)

Maintain records of:
- Processing operations performed
- Purpose of processing
- Categories of data subjects
- Recipients of personal information
- Data transfers (cross-border)
- Security measures implemented
- Retention periods
- Audit logs (minimum 5 years)
