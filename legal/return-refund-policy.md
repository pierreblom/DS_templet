## 2. Your Consumer Rights (CPA Compliance)

### 2.1 Cooling-Off Period (CPA Section 16)

You have the **right to return any product within 7 (seven) days** of receiving it, without providing a reason.

**Important Conditions:**
- Products must be in original, unused condition
- Original packaging and tags must be intact
- Hygiene seals must be unbroken (for intimate apparel)
- You are responsible for return shipping costs
- Original delivery costs are not refunded

### 2.2 Defective Products (CPA Section 20 & 56)

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect product, you have the right to:
- A full refund (including delivery costs)
- A replacement product at no additional cost
- Repair (if applicable and agreed)

**We cover all return shipping costs for defective or incorrect items.**

### 2.3 Warranty Period

Products are covered against manufacturing defects for **30 days** from the date of receipt.

## 3. Eligibility for Returns

### 3.1 Returnable Products

The following products can be returned within 7 days if conditions are met:
- All bras with hygiene seal intact
- All shapewear with hygiene seal intact
- All apparel with original tags attached
- All products in unused, resalable condition

### 3.2 Non-Returnable Products

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following **cannot be returned** once packaging/seals are broken:

- **Intimate Apparel:** If hygiene seal is broken and item is not defective
- **Final Sale Items:** Clearly marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
- **Gift Cards:** Non-returnable once issued
- **Worn or Washed Items:** Unless defective
- **Items Without Original Packaging:** Unless defective

### 3.3 Exceptions for Defective Products

Even if normally non-returnable, you can return products that are:
- Damaged on arrival
- Defective or faulty
- Significantly different from the description
- Incorrect item sent

## 4. How to Return a Product

### Step 1: Contact Us

Email us at **returns@shopbeha.com** within 7 days of receiving your order.

**Include the Following Information:**
- Order number
- Product name(s) and SKU(s)
- Reason for return
- Photos (if defective or damaged)
- Preferred resolution (refund or exchange)

### Step 2: Receive Return Authorisation

We will review your request and respond within **2 business days** with:
- A Return Authorisation Number (RAN)
- Return shipping instructions
- Return address

**Important:** Do not ship returns without a RAN. Unauthorised returns may not be processed.

### Step 3: Package Your Return

- **Include:** Return Authorisation Number (printed and placed inside package)
- **Ensure:** Product is securely packaged to prevent damage during transit
- **Include:** All original packaging, tags, and accessories
- **Remove:** Any personal items from the package

**Packaging Tips:**
- Use a sturdy box or padded envelope
- Wrap products individually if returning multiple items
- Use bubble wrap or paper for cushioning
- Seal package securely with tape

### Step 4: Ship Your Return

**Shipping Options:**

1. **Own Courier (Customer Pays):**
   - Use any courier service (Pargo, Aramex, The Courier Guy, PostNet, etc.)
   - Ship to the address provided in your RAN email
   - **Keep your tracking number** as proof of postage
   - We recommend using a trackable service

2. **Collection Service (Customer Pays):**
   - We can arrange courier collection (fees apply)
   - Request collection when contacting us
   - Collection fees will be deducted from your refund

**Return Shipping Costs:**
- **Change of Mind Returns:** Customer pays return shipping
- **Defective/Incorrect Items:** ShopBeha pays return shipping (we'll provide a prepaid waybill)

### Step 5: Confirmation and Processing

- We will confirm receipt of your return via email
- Inspection takes 1-2 business days
- Refund or exchange is processed within 7 business days

## 5. Refund Process

### 5.1 Inspection

Upon receiving your return, we will:
1. Inspect the product(s) within 1-2 business days
2. Verify that return conditions are met
3. Approve or decline the refund

**If Approved:**
- Refund is processed immediately
- You receive email confirmation

**If Declined:**
- We will contact you with the reason
- Products will be returned to you at your cost
- Common reasons: hygiene seal broken, product worn/washed, tags removed

### 5.2 Refund Amount

**Change of Mind Returns:**
- Product price is refunded
- Original delivery costs are **not** refunded
- Return shipping costs are your responsibility

**Defective/Incorrect Items:**
- Full product price refunded
- Original delivery costs refunded
- Return shipping costs refunded or prepaid

**Formula:** Refund Amount = Product Price - Applicable Deductions

### 5.3 Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**:

| Payment Method | Refund Method | Processing Time |
|---------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Credit Card | Credit to same card | 7-10 business days |
| Debit Card | Credit to same card | 7-10 business days |
| Instant EFT | Bank transfer to source account | 5-7 business days |

**Note:** Bank processing times vary and are beyond our control.

### 5.4 Refund Timeline

| Stage | Timeframe |
|-------|-----------|
| Return shipment to us | Varies (1-5 days depending on courier) |
| Inspection | 1-2 business days after receipt |
| Refund processing | 7 business days after approval |
| Bank processing | 5-10 business days (varies by bank) |
| **Total estimated time** | **2-3 weeks from return shipment** |

### 5.5 Tracking Your Refund

- You will receive email confirmation when refund is processed
- Check with your bank if refund is not visible within expected timeframe
- Contact us at returns@shopbeha.com if issues arise

## 6. Exchanges

### 6.1 How to Exchange

We do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange a product:

1. **Return the original product** (follow return process above)
2. **Place a new order** for the desired product
3. **Wait for refund** before placing new order, or
4. **Place new order immediately** at your discretion

**Why this process?**
- Ensures you get the replacement product quickly
- Avoids stock availability issues
- Simplifies processing for faster resolution

### 6.2 Size Exchanges

For size exchanges:
- Check our [Size Guide](../size-guide.md) before ordering
- Follow the return and re-order process above
- Consider ordering multiple sizes and returning unwanted items (within 7-day window)

### 6.3 Colour Exchanges

For colour exchanges:
- Note that colours may vary slightly from screen display
- Follow the return and re-order process above

## 7. Damaged or Defective Products

### 7.1 Reporting Damage

Report damaged or defective products **within 7 days** of receipt:

**Email:** returns@shopbeha.com  
**Subject:** "Damaged/Defective Product - Order #[Order Number]"

**Include:**
- Order number
- Product details
- Clear photos showing:
  - Defect or damage
  - Product packaging
  - Shipping label (if package was damaged)
- Description of the issue

### 7.2 Our Response

We will review your claim within 2 business days and provide:
- Confirmation of defect/damage
- Prepaid return shipping label (if return required)
- Choice of full refund or replacement

**No need to return if:**
- Defect is minor and we can offer a partial refund
- Product is unusable and cannot be returned for hygiene reasons

### 7.3 Manufacturing Defects

Covered defects include:
- Faulty stitching or seams
- Fabric defects (holes, tears, discolouration)
- Hardware failures (broken clasps, zippers, hooks)
- Elastic failure (within 30 days)

Not covered:
- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from improper use or washing
- Alterations or modifications
- Damage after 30 days (unless covered by extended warranty)

## 8. Incorrect Products

### 8.1 If You Receive Wrong Item

If you receive an incorrect product:

1. **Do not open hygiene seals** (if possible)
2. **Contact us immediately** at returns@shopbeha.com
3. **Provide photos** of the product and packaging
4. **Keep all packaging** until resolution

### 8.2 Resolution

We will:
- Provide prepaid return shipping
- Send the correct product immediately (if in stock)
- Offer full refund if correct product is unavailable
- Cover all costs associated with the error

## 9. Lost or Undelivered Orders

### 9.1 Order Not Received

If your order has not arrived within expected delivery timeframe:

1. **Check tracking information** provided in shipping confirmation email
2. **Wait an additional 2 business days** (couriers may be delayed)
3. **Contact us** at support@shopbeha.com if still not received

### 9.2 Investigation Process

We will:
- Contact the courier to investigate
- Request proof of delivery
- Wait 7-10 business days for courier investigation
- Process refund or reship if package is confirmed lost

### 9.3 Refund for Lost Packages

If package is confirmed lost:
- Full refund issued (including delivery costs), or
- Replacement product shipped at no cost
- Processing time: 7-10 business days after confirmation

## 10. Special Circumstances

### 10.1 Partial Returns

If you ordered multiple items:
- You can return some items and keep others
- Each item must meet return conditions
- Refund is calculated per item

### 10.2 Gift Returns

If you received a product as a gift:
- You can return it within 7 days
- Refund will be issued to the original purchaser's payment method
- Or we can issue a store credit for the purchase amount

### 10.3 International Orders

We currently only deliver within South Africa. If you received an order from overseas:
- Contact us immediately at returns@shopbeha.com
- We will provide special return instructions

## 11. Refund Delays

### 11.1 Common Reasons for Delays

- Bank processing times (5-10 days)
- Public holidays affecting business days
- Courier delays in return shipment
- Incomplete return information
- Product fails inspection

### 11.2 If Your Refund Is Delayed

Contact us at returns@shopbeha.com with:
- Order number
- RAN (Return Authorisation Number)
- Date return was shipped
- Tracking number

We will investigate and provide an update within 2 business days.

## 12. Abusive Returns Policy

### 12.1 Fair Use

We trust our customers, but we reserve the right to:
- Limit or decline returns from customers with excessive return rates
- Refuse returns that do not meet our policy conditions
- Take action against fraudulent return activity

### 12.2 What Constitutes Abuse

- Returning worn or damaged products as "defective"
- Removing tags and returning as "wrong size"
- Excessive returns (>50% of orders)
- Returning products after using them for events

### 12.3 Our Commitment

We will:
- Communicate concerns with you first
- Provide opportunity to discuss issues
- Only take action in clear cases of policy abuse
